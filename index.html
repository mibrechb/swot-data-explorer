Welcome to the SWOT Lake and River Explorer

This tool lets you explore global hydrologic measurements for lakes and rivers from the Surface Water and Ocean Topography (SWOT) mission. Measurements are derived from high-resolution radar observations collected by the Ka-band Radar Interferometer (KaRIn) onboard SWOT. The provided data is served through the PO.DAAC Hydrocron API. Start exploring by selecting a lake or river reach/node, or by searching for a location using the search tool in the top left lower corner.

This tool was developed as part of the SOS-Water EU Horizon project. Check out sos-water.eu for more information.
